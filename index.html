01

What we work on.
The whole toolbox.

Four pillars
Every session
SKL·01

Receiving & Framing

Quiet hands, strong glove path, and stealing strikes at the edges. We build a low, athletic setup that umps — and pitchers — trust.

SKL·02

Blocking

Kill the ball, keep it close, recover fast. Reps on angles, chest position, and the mentality that nothing gets past you.

SKL·03

Throwing & Footwork

Clean exchanges, efficient transfers, and footwork that shaves tenths off your throw down. Accuracy first, then speed.

SKL·04

Game IQ

Calling pitches, controlling the run game, and leading the field. The stuff that makes coaches hand you the job.

What ages do you coach?
Ages 8–15, from first-time catchers to travel-ball starters. Every lesson is 1-on-1, so the plan fits the player — not the other way around.
What should my player bring?
Full catcher's gear if you have it (helmet, chest protector, shin guards), a catcher's mitt or fielding glove, cleats or turf shoes, and a water bottle. Don't have gear yet? Text ahead — we can usually work something out.
What about weather or rescheduling?
Life happens. Reschedules are free with 24-hour notice — just text Aiden. If weather cancels a lesson, we move it to the next open slot at no charge.
Where are lessons held?
Local fields and cages around Greensboro, NC. The exact location comes with your confirmation email once your payment is verified.
